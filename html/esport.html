INDY 500

Known as “The Greatest Spectacle in Racing,” the INDY 500 is one of the most prestigious race events in the world, held since 1911 at Indianapolis Motor Speedway. It is part of the INDYCAR Series in the United States.

Watkins Glen 6 HR

The Watkins Glen 6 Hour is a 6 hour endurance race held annually at Watkins Glen International in Watkins Glen, New York. It is the fifth round of the IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship.

Brickyard 400

The Brickyard 400 returns to the iRacing Special Event Calendar in 2024. Indianapolis Motor Speedway has played host to the Brickyard 400 in the NASCAR Cup series since 1994. It's the third of four full-length events in the NASCAR iRacing Series Schedule.
